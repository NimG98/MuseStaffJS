Dynamically modify notes

You can add and modify the notes in the staff!

Wide range of usability

  • Can be used in web apps that provide music sheets for a wide variety of songs, so that users can follow the notes or lyrics and learn how to play the songs.

  • Can use the modifiable staff if you, the developer, want to create your own melodies. Or, if you want the user of your app to design melodies themselves.

  • Teach music staff theory and give examples of different notes, time signatures, and other musical theory concepts.
